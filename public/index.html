Florence's Music & Entertainment Hub

Florence, Alabama is home to a thriving live music scene and award-winning restaurants. Nestled in Alabama's historic Shoals region near the Tennessee River, Florence offers an exceptional blend of Southern hospitality, world-class entertainment, and culinary excellence. Whether you're catching live performances at local venues or dining at celebrated restaurants, Florence delivers authentic experiences in the heart of the Tennessee Valley.
