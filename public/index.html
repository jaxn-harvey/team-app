🎵 The Muscle Shoals Legacy

Muscle Shoals is the birthplace of legendary soul music. FAME Studios and the world-renowned Swampers session musicians created timeless masterpieces here that changed music forever. Artists like Aretha Franklin, Wilson Pickett, and Etta James recorded their greatest hits overlooking the Tennessee River. Today, that same creative spirit flows through our venues and restaurants.
